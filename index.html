Born at Mount Saint Vincent University

SmartSip began as a strategic project within the Business and Tourism Department at MSVU. Developed for an Integrated Marketing Communications course under Dr. Karen Blotnicky, our mission is to solve the hydration gap that many university students face during demanding academic routines. We believe that staying hydrated shouldn't just be a health habit—it should be a productivity strategy.
